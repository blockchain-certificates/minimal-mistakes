## Design principles

- Open source
- Open standards
- Open participation
- Recipient control
- Simplicity
- Minimize resource requirements for adopters (computational, cost..)
- Solution must be viable without any proprietary product
- Blockchain agnostic in the long term
- Maximize confidence in the end product

## Out of scope

- Identity
  - association is formed through public keys owned by issuer/recipient
  - could be an extension
- Registration/discovery of issuers
